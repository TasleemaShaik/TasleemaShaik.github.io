About Me

Hi, I'm Tasleema Shaik Mohammed, I’m a full-stack engineer with 5+ years building and operating production systems across Go, Python, PostgreSQL, and all major cloud providers(AWS, GCP, Azure). I’ve shipped APIs and data pipelines end-to-end (alerting integrations, autoscaling services, Postgres read-replica balancing with PGPOOL2), led debugging and observability efforts (Helm rollout fixes, runbooks, CI/CD), and care about turning customer needs into fast, reliable releases. Recent projects include PathSpotter.AI (geospatial assistant) and ExAdvisor(An LLM based privacy-first chatbot that helps you decide if and how to reach out to someone from your past). I enjoy owning roadmaps with product, defining clear success metrics, and scaling backends to millions of requests while keeping latency, cost, and correctness in check.
